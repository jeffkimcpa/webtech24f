Welcome! A place where you can get to know Japan!

(click on the banner image for a surprise!)

Japan is an islandic country, located in East-Asia with a current population of 125.1 million. Having a rich culture, scenic nature, and tasty cuisine Japan has been a popular destination for tourists around the world, which has about 35 million tourists annually. In being such a popular destination, Japan has been keen on preserving their historical landmarks and artifacts. Due to these efforts, Japan has countless cities that integrates both modern, urban buildings as well as old, historic buildings, wherein the contrast makes it even more beautiful. This website aims to provide my personal favorite attractions in three different cities, which also includes an itinerary builder based upon my database, which would provide the visitors to my website adequate knowledge and planning before traveling to Japan. I hope my website helps your visit! Thanks.
